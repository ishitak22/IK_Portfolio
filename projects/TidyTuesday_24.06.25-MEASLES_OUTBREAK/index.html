Key Insights

  • Seasonal patterns show distinct outbreak peaks across regions, supporting the need for region-specific vaccination timing.

  • High-incidence countries like Madagascar, Mongolia, and Kyrgyzstan had relatively low total case counts, suggesting possible under-reporting.

  • The scatter plot revealed several countries with inconsistent surveillance, highlighting a gap in global health reporting systems.

  • Countries with low vaccine coverage and large populations ‚Äî such as Nigeria and Pakistan ‚Äî are critical priorities for public health interventions.
